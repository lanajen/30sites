What is a factorial?

A factorial is the product any given non-negative integer (whole number) and every positive integer less than it

For example:

5! = 120, because 5! = 5 * 4 * 3 * 2 * 1

And:

4! = 4 * 3 * 2 * 1 = 24

3! = 3 * 2 * 1 = 6

2! = 2 * 1 = 2

1! = 1

But:

0! = 1 (this is special, it is a rule)
