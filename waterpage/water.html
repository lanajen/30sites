David H. Paul, Inc. (DHP)

is a high-tech water treatment training and consulting company.

Since 1988 DHP has been the world's leader in providing training on high-tech water treatment
technologies. We were the first to introduce an "Associates of Applied Science" degree for high-tech
water treatment. Now we have many more training programs and have provided technical consulting
to facilities all around the world.

Our expertise allows us to train beginners, operators and engineers. We have the knowledge you
need to excel in any high-tech water treatment job. We help to save facilities costs and damage to
equipment.
